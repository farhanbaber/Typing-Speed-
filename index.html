The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog, but this time it was not in a hurry. In 2025, people still use typewriters for fun, even though laptops and tablets are faster. Typing is not just about speed; accuracy is equally important. If you can type 80 words per minute with 95% accuracy, you are already better than most people. Practice every day, stay relaxed, and keep your fingers close to the home row keys
